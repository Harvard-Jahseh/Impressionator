What is Impressionator?

Impressionator is a web application that will make text impressions of people. Whether it be a celebrity or a close friend, Impressionator will find that person's speech habits and patterns, then produce something based on that information. All you need to do is give it transcriptions of how the person talks, and let Impressionator do the work!

More is Better

The ML process does better with more data. The more text you give us, the better we'll do!

Smart and Efficient Learning

The neural network will integrate your data in its training, but it's not completely relient on it, making it both smarter and faster.
